ABC ALGOL - Wikipedia ABC ALGOL From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search ABC ALGOL is an extension of the programming language ALGOL 60 with arbitrary data structures and user-defined operators, intended for computer algebra (symbolic mathematics). Despite its advances, it was never used as widely as Algol proper.
